## Why Continuous Delivery matters for agile teams

Continuous Delivery enables agile teams to release software more frequently and reliably, ensuring that new features and fixes can be delivered to users quickly. This practice fosters a culture of continuous improvement and responsiveness to customer feedback, ultimately enhancing product quality and team collaboration.
